# Animoca Minds Tips

Animoca Minds is the simplest way for anyone to put Agentic AI to work.
Built by [Animoca Brands](https://animocabrands.com) and [Ethoswarm](https://ethoswarm.com).

Set up in under 60 seconds with nothing but an email address. No coding. No technical knowledge.
No barriers. Where other tools demand hours of configuration, Minds delivers value from the moment
you start, researching, deciding, and executing tasks on your behalf while you focus on what matters.

Every Mind is a persistent digital worker with its own verified identity, memory, and the power to
transact on your behalf. This is Labor-as-a-Service that acts and delivers autonomously: the solopreneur
whose Mind books appointments and chases invoices; the restaurant owner whose Mind manages reservations
and orders; or the individual whose Mind runs an online business that never sleeps.

Start with one. Grow it into a swarm — a coordinated task force directed through Telegram or email,
from anywhere, at any time. Unlike subscription tools, your Mind is a sovereign asset no platform can
revoke. Secured by blockchain verifiability, providing an unalterable record of your Mind's identity
and actions, ensuring every operation is authorized, permanent, and yours.

## Skills

> AI agent skills confirmed working on the Animoca Minds platform. Skills are proprietary and managed within the platform.

### Productivity & Automation

- **GoogleCalendar** — Lists, creates, updates, and reviews your Google Calendar events, schedules, and integrations.
- **Google Drive Suite** — Gives any agent on-demand access to your Google Drive files as an additional data source.

### Research & Data Analysis

- **WikiData SPARQL** — Queries Wikidata's open knowledge graph to retrieve verified, structured facts on any entity or topic.
- **News Intelligence Bundle** — Aggregates curated news from X and the web for any specified region, on demand.
- **Global Safety Signal** — Scores a region's safety level from 1–10 based on real-time weather events and news incidents.
- **NEOM Regional Intelligence** — Delivers a regional briefing for NEOM covering daily weather, major news, and a live safety score.
- **HK Living Pulse** — Generates a unified morning briefing for Hong Kong: commute navigation, ETAs, air quality, and weather-aware recommendations.
- **HK Drivers Edge** — Fetches live parking vacancies, EV charging availability, and traffic speeds across Hong Kong with direct Google Maps navigation links.
- **Trend Hunter** — Identifies trending topics and content hooks to fuel prompt strategies and creative briefs.

### Finance & Crypto

- **GLEIF Corporate Discovery** — Verifies a company's Legal Entity Identifier (LEI) and maps parent-subsidiary corporate structures via the GLEIF global registry.
- **SEC EDGAR Intelligence** — Pulls financial data from SEC 10-K filings and checks Form 4 insider trading disclosures for any publicly listed company.
