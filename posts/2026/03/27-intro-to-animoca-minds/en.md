# Introducing Animoca Minds

Want an AI that just answers questions? Cute. But what if you could have a whole squad of AI agents that research, create, and execute while you relax? That's Animoca Minds.

Animoca Minds is a first-of-its-kind agentic AI platform where every AI agent — called a Mind — has its own verified identity, persistent memory, and the ability to act on your behalf. No coding, no technical setup, no barriers. Just results.

## What Is a Mind?

A Mind is not a chatbot. It's a persistent digital worker with its own identity and memory. Every Mind:

- **Has a verified identity** — each Mind is uniquely identifiable and sovereign
- **Retains memory** — your Mind remembers past interactions and builds on them
- **Operates autonomously** — set it a task and it executes without constant prompting
- **Can be equipped with skills** — expand your Mind's capabilities with specialized tools

Unlike one-off AI tools, your Mind is a sovereign digital asset — no platform can revoke it, and it works for you continuously.

## Why Animoca Minds?

Most AI tools leave you doing all the heavy lifting. You ask a question, get an answer, and have to figure out what to do with it yourself. Animoca Minds flips this entirely.

With Animoca Minds, you set the direction. Your Mind takes over, executes, and comes back with results. It provides labor-as-a-service: autonomous execution without the overhead of managing technical infrastructure.

The result? You stop prompting and start delegating.

## Getting Started in Under 60 Seconds

Awakening your first Mind is as simple as sending an email:

1. Visit [animocaminds.ai](https://app.animocaminds.ai)
2. Enter your email address
3. Reply to the welcome message from your Concierge AI
4. Your Mind is awakened and ready to work

No coding required. No technical knowledge. No barriers. Within a minute, you have an autonomous AI agent working for you.

## Grow Your Swarm

One Mind is powerful. A swarm of Minds is transformative.

As your needs grow, you can create multiple specialized Minds — each focused on a different domain. Research, content creation, market monitoring, customer service — each Mind operates in its own lane, and together they form an autonomous digital workforce that operates around the clock.
