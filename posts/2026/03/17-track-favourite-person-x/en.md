# Track Your Favourite Person on X (Twitter) with Animoca Minds

Keeping up with multiple X accounts, sometimes in different languages? Let your Mind do the heavy lifting and help you focus on what matters.

With **Animoca Minds**, you can awaken a Mind to track accounts, translate posts, and deliver updates exactly how you want — no stress, no extra work. No coding. No confusing setup. Just clear instructions, and your Mind takes care of the rest.

## Getting Started: A Quick Overview

The process is simple:

1. Create your Animoca Mind with the following instruction: *"Create a Mind that helps to track '[Your favourite person]' Twitter posts."*
2. Give your Mind a name and configure other details based on the setup instructions.
3. Wait for your Mind to be ready.
4. After your Mind is awakened, set the requirements and expected items you want in the report. For example:
   - Report once per day
   - Report your findings every day at 00:00 UTC
   - In the report, analyze the most viewed and most liked posts of the day
   - Summarize the tweets
5. Enjoy your new Animoca Mind!

## Phase 1: Creating and Naming Your Animoca Mind

The first step is to instantiate your AI agent — your Animoca Mind — and define its core purpose.

1. **Initiate Mind Creation:** Begin the process of creating a new Animoca Mind within the platform.
2. **Define the Mind's Core Function:** Clearly articulate the AI's objective using a precise and directive sentence. The instruction for this Mind must explicitly outline its tracking goal:
   - *"Create a Mind that is dedicated to tracking, analyzing, and reporting on the daily X (Twitter) posts of '[Your favourite person/account's exact X Handle]'."*
3. **Configure and Name Your Mind:**
   - **Naming:** Assign a clear, descriptive name to your new Mind (e.g., "ElonTracker," "CompetitorWatchDog," or "ThoughtLeaderDigest"). This makes it easy to manage among potentially many other Minds.
   - **Setting Details:** Complete any remaining configuration details as specified in the accompanying setup instructions.

## Phase 2: Awaiting Mind Activation

Once the initial parameters are set, your Animoca Mind will enter a brief activation phase as it processes its core directive and prepares its operational framework.

1. **Wait for Readiness:** Allow a short period for your Mind to fully initialize and connect to the necessary data streams.
2. **Mind Awakening Notification:** You will receive a notification once your Mind is *Awakened* and ready to accept its specific reporting and analysis requirements.

## Phase 3: Setting Reporting Requirements and Analysis Depth

After your Animoca Mind is *Awakened*, define the precise parameters for its daily operation, analysis, and final output.

### A. Defining Report Frequency and Timing

- **Frequency:** Instruct the Mind to compile and deliver a comprehensive report **once per 24-hour cycle**.
- **Delivery Time:** Specify the exact moment for report generation using Universal Coordinated Time (UTC): *"Report your findings every day precisely at 00:00 UTC."*

### B. Defining Report Content and Analytical Depth

1. **Key Post Identification:** The Mind systematically scans all posts published by the target account within the 24-hour reporting period:
   - **Most Viewed Analysis:** Identify the single post that garnered the highest number of views or impressions during that day.
   - **Most Engaged Analysis:** Identify the single post that received the highest count of Likes during the day.
2. **In-Depth Post Analysis:** For both the "Most Viewed" and "Most Liked" posts, the Mind provides a brief contextual analysis explaining *why* that post likely achieved such high metrics (e.g., topic relevance, use of media, time of posting, or engagement with prominent accounts).
3. **Comprehensive Daily Summary:** The Mind synthesizes the target's entire day of X activity into a concise, well-structured summary capturing the main themes, sentiment shifts, and overall focus of the target's tweeting.
