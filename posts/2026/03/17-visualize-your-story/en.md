# Visualize Your Story with Animoca Minds

It's time to visualize your story like never before. With **Animoca Minds**, you don't just use a chatbot — you awaken a Mind: an autonomous agent that turns your ideas into a full visual storyboard, automatically.

Not a chatbot. Not a one-shot prompt tool. An AI agent that works for you and keeps building until the job is done.

## Getting Started: A Quick Overview

The process is simple:

1. Create your Animoca Mind with the following instruction: *"A Mind that helps turn my story into a visual storyboard for upcoming film production."*
2. Equip your Mind with the **Storyboard Image Generator** tool.
3. Wait for your Mind to be ready.
4. After your Mind is awakened, set the direction and style for your storyboard.
5. Let your Mind execute, iterate, and expand your vision autonomously.

## Step 1: Awaken Your Mind

The first step is to create and awaken your AI agent — your Animoca Mind.

- **For new users:** Go to [animocaminds.ai](https://app.animocaminds.ai), enter your email, and follow the Concierge AI's instructions to create your Mind.
- **For existing users:** Ask your Concierge AI to create a new Mind.

**Pro tip:** Set your Mind's purpose clearly from the start:
> *"A Mind that helps turn my story into a visual storyboard for upcoming film production."*

A well-defined purpose ensures your Mind is focused and effective from the moment it awakens.

## Step 2: Equip Your Mind

Once your Mind is awakened, equip it with the right capability:

- **Storyboard Image Generator** (Tool ID: `5EF4768E-3F1D-F111-AD1D-0EA9A5017E89`)

With this tool installed, your Mind can transform narrative ideas into visual storyboard panels — automatically.

## Step 3: Shape Your Mind

Define the creative direction for your storyboard. Tell your Mind what you want:

- Your story (the narrative, characters, or scenes)
- Your preferred visual style, for example:
  - ✏️ Rough pencil sketch
  - 💧 Digital watercolor
  - ✒️ Comic-style illustration

You define the direction. Your Mind takes it from there and generates visuals automatically.

**Guide, don't grind.** Step in only when needed — give feedback, adjust direction, and refine the flow. Your Mind adapts and improves, building the storyboard exactly how you envision it.

## Step 4: Automate and Expand

Once your Mind is set up, trigger the loop and let it work. You can instruct it to:

- *"Ask me which story to build next daily"*
- *"Generate 3 scene ideas for me to choose"*
- *"Continue the storyboard automatically"*

Your Mind will:

- Prompt you to decide the direction
- Continuously build and expand your storyboard
- Execute, iterate, and keep building — all on its own

You lead the direction. Your Mind runs the execution loop.

## What Makes This Different

With Animoca Minds, your agentic assistant doesn't just respond — it **acts, builds, and evolves** with your creative direction. Instead of manually prompting a chatbot for every panel, you set the vision once and let your Mind do the heavy lifting.

Whether you're working on a film, a comic series, a game narrative, or any visual story format, Animoca Minds gives you a creative collaborator that works tirelessly in the background.
