# Set Up Your Animoca Mind as a Personalized Meal Planner

Unlock the power of AI to streamline your nutrition journey. The Animoca Mind Meal Planner leverages advanced agentic capabilities to generate customized meal ideas, create practical shopping lists, and track your nutritional goals — all tailored to your dietary preferences and restrictions.

Whether you follow a keto plan, eat vegan, manage food allergies, or simply want to eat more intentionally, your Mind can become an on-demand AI chef designed entirely around your needs.

## What You Need Before You Start

Getting set up only takes a few minutes. Before you begin, have these ready:

- An active [Animoca Mind](https://app.animocaminds.ai) account
- Your basic dietary information — allergies, preferences (e.g., vegetarian, keto, Mediterranean), and health or fitness goals

Once you have those details, you're ready to turn your Mind into a personalized meal planning partner.

## Step 1: Awaken Your Mind

If you haven't created your Mind yet, it takes under 60 seconds. Visit [animocaminds.ai](https://app.animocaminds.ai), enter your email, and reply to your Concierge AI's welcome message. Your Mind will be ready immediately.

Already have a Mind? Skip straight to Step 2.

## Step 2: Equip the Meal Planner Skill

Tell your Mind to equip the **Meal Planner (Elite Chef Edition)** skill:

> *"Equip yourself with Meal Planner (Elite Chef Edition)"*

Your Mind will equip the skill automatically and confirm when it's ready.

## Step 3: Set Up Your Personalized Plan

Now give your Mind the context it needs to build a plan that works for you:

**Plan duration:** How long do you want the meal plan to run? A week? A month?

**Your location:** Since the plan sources from local market availability, let your Mind know which region or country you're in.

**Dietary preferences — the important part:**
- Any **allergies** (e.g., gluten, nuts, shellfish)
- Any **restrictions** (ingredients or cooking methods to avoid)
- Your **dietary style** (Standard, Vegan, Low-Carb, Keto, Mediterranean, etc.)
- Your **health or fitness goals** (weight loss, muscle gain, balanced nutrition)

The more context you give your Mind, the more precisely tailored your plan will be.

## Step 4: Generate Your First Meal Plan

Once your Mind has all the details, tell it to generate your first meal plan:

> *"Generate my first meal plan based on the preferences I gave you."*

Review what it produces and don't hesitate to ask for changes — your Mind adapts in real time.

## What Your Meal Planner Mind Can Do

Your Meal Planner Mind acts as an intelligent agent, not a static app. Here's what it handles automatically:

- **Learns your preferences** — remembers your dietary restrictions, favourite cuisines, and cooking habits across sessions
- **Automates weekly meal planning** — curates a diverse menu with comprehensive recipes so you never fall into repetitive routines
- **Generates instant grocery lists** — turns your meal plan into an organized shopping list, solving the grocery guesswork instantly
- **Tracks nutritional goals** — keeps your macros and targets in view as it plans each meal
